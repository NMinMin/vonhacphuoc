Astronomy

Maxwell Scientific provides a wide range of science education and science demonstration products that are perfect for the home or the classroom. Choose from the finest supplies, and videos available to create interest and excitement in your students.

  • Astronomy:

    The finest equipment from Newton, DC Optics, and StarVision to help teach the wonders of astronomy.

  • Chemistry:

    40 chemistry kits, developed especially for Maxwell Scientific by the Chemistry department at Princeton University.

  • Electronics:

    A great selection of kits and wide variety of interesting and fun elelctronic projects.

  • Engineering:

    A wide variety of educational material to teach basic engineering principles and supplies to help your students design the best structures.

  • Physics:

    Maxwell provides a number of wonderful kits to help teach physics in fun and interesting ways.
